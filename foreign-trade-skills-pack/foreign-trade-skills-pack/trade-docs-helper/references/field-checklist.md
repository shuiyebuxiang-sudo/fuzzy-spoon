# Field Checklist

## PI / Proforma Invoice

Must have:

- Seller and buyer names.
- Product model/name.
- Quantity.
- Unit price and currency.
- Total amount.
- Payment term.
- Trade term.
- Delivery time.

Nice to have:

- PI number.
- Validity date.
- Bank account.
- Packing details.
- Shipping destination.

## CI / Commercial Invoice

Must have:

- Seller and buyer names.
- Invoice number and date.
- Product model/name.
- Quantity.
- Unit price and total value.
- Currency.
- Trade term.
- Country of origin.
- Shipping information if known.

## PO / Purchase Order

Must have:

- Buyer and seller names.
- PO number and date.
- Product model/name.
- Quantity.
- Agreed price.
- Delivery requirement.
- Payment term.

## Certificate of Origin

Must have:

- Exporter.
- Consignee.
- Product description.
- Quantity/weight.
- Country of origin.
- Invoice number.
- Transport details if known.

## Customs Declaration

Use when goods are ready to ship or the user/forwarder asks for declaration materials.

Commonly needed:

- Chinese and English product name.
- HS code.
- Material/use/brand/model.
- Quantity, unit, gross weight, net weight.
- Declared value and currency.
- Origin country.
- Destination country.
- Packing type and package count.
- Trade term.
